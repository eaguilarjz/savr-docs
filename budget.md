# Budget

The Budget page is the heart of savr. Each month you decide how every dollar is spent before it leaves your account.

## Reading the budget

| Column    | What it shows                                             |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Category  | The spending category                                     |
| Target    | Your goal for this category (if set)                      |
| Budgeted  | How much you assigned this month                          |
| Spent     | Actual spending recorded so far                           |
| Available | Budgeted minus Spent (carries rollover from prior months) |

The **To Be Budgeted** banner at the top shows how much income is still unassigned. It turns red if you've over-assigned.

---

## Assigning money

Click any amount in the **Budgeted** column and type a new value. Press Enter or click away to save.

### Fill to target

Click the **↓** button on a category to automatically fill it based on:

- **Underfunded** — tops up to the target amount
- **Assigned last month** — copies whatever you assigned last month
- **Spent last month** — matches your actual spending from last month

### Auto-Assign

The **Auto-Assign** button (top right of the budget table) fills all categories at once. Choose a strategy and savr will cap each category at your **To Be Budgeted** balance so you never go over.

### Fill by Targets

Fills all categories that have a target set to their target amount in one step.

---

## Moving money

If you overspend in a category and don't want to add more income, move money from a category that has surplus.

1. Click the transfer icon (⇄) on the destination category.
2. Select the source category.
3. Enter the amount to move.

### Covering overspending

When a category goes negative (red), a **Cover** button appears. Click it to pick another category to pull funds from.

---

## Targets

A target tells savr how much a category should receive. Targets power auto-fill and show you at a glance whether a category is on track.

### Setting a target

Click **Set target** (or the existing target badge) on any category. Choose a type:

| Type    | Description                          |
| ------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Monthly | A fixed amount each month            |
| Weekly  | Multiplied by weeks in the month     |
| Yearly  | Divided evenly across 12 months      |
| Custom  | A specific amount by a specific date |

Enable **Rollover** if unspent money should carry forward (good for irregular expenses like annual subscriptions).

---

## Spending transactions

Click any amount in the **Spent** column to see all transactions charged to that category this month.

---

## Budget history

Click the clock icon on a category to review every allocation and money move made in that category this month.

---

## Navigating months

Use the **← →** arrows to move between months, or click **This month** to jump back to the current month. Past months are read-only — you can review them but not edit allocations.

---

## Hidden categories and groups

You can hide categories or groups you don't use often without deleting them. Go to **Categories** to hide or unhide items. On the Budget page, click **Show hidden** (appears when hidden items exist) to temporarily reveal them.
